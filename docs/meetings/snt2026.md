## Location
Hans-Dickmann-Kolleg (HaDiKo), Klosterweg 28, 76131 Karlsruhe

### Arrival by public transit

- From the Karlsruhe main station, take the tram 3 (Rintheim) or tram 2 (Wolfartsweier) to Durlacher Tor / KIT-Campus-Süd. 
- Once you have reached Durlacher Tor, take the bus 30 (Elbinger Straße) until you have reached Büchiger Alle. 
- From there, it's only a few steps to HaDiKo.
